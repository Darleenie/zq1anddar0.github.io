Other Platforms

Aqara, Sengled, Google Home/Nest, and Alexa don't have open device-control APIs for personal web apps. The easiest way to control them here is to connect them to SmartThings — once connected, they appear automatically in the device list above.

  • Aqara → SmartThings (via Matter or direct Zigbee hub integration)
  • Sengled → SmartThings (via Sengled Hub or Zigbee integration)
  • Google Nest → SmartThings (via Google Home cloud integration)
  • Alexa routines → Set up in Alexa app, trigger from SmartThings events
